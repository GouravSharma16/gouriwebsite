Self-Watering Plant

In order to recycle plastics and making something useful for the environment. Self watering plant could be a good example.

A Short Brief About The Product

  1. This is a Self-watering plant which can absorb water from the woolen cloth and transfer it to the plastic portion with soil.
  2. This product will be made up of 2 cutted portions of a plastic bottle. The uppermost portion of the plastic bottle will be kept upside down with soil and a plant in it
  3. The down part will have water and a woolen cloth. The woolen cloth will absorb water and continuously transfer water to the plastic half above.
  • This would be helpful for both humans and the environment. Humans would not need to water the plant every day Planting trees is therefore good for the environment as well.

    • -It has many functions like

    • planting more trees
    • home decor
    • put water only once in weeks
    • good example of “ best out of waste”
    • uses of old cloth and used plastic bottle
